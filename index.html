FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Smart Business Idea Generator Agent.

What IBM technologies power this platform? ▾
The platform is built on IBM watsonx.ai as the core AI runtime, IBM Granite foundation models for language understanding and generation, IBM Langflow for visual AI workflow orchestration, and IBM Orchestrate for multi-agent automation. These work together to provide enterprise-grade reasoning, reliability, and scalability.
What inputs can I provide to generate ideas? ▾
IdeaGenAI supports fully multimodal inputs — you can type a text prompt, upload a document (PDF, DOCX), attach images or product sketches, submit a voice note, or even paste a URL. The AI will process all modalities together to understand your full context.
What does each generated business idea include? ▾
Each generated idea includes: a business concept summary, problem statement, proposed solution, target audience profile, revenue models, market opportunity estimate (TAM/SAM/SOM), feasibility analysis, SWOT analysis, innovation score (1–10), implementation roadmap with milestones, and competitive landscape overview.
How does the Agentic AI layer work? ▾
The Agentic AI layer uses IBM Orchestrate to deploy autonomous agents that run in parallel: a Research Agent scrapes recent papers and news, a Market Agent pulls trend data, a Scoring Agent evaluates feasibility and innovation, and a Synthesis Agent compiles the final structured output. No manual intervention is needed.
Can I refine or iterate on generated ideas? ▾
Yes! IdeaGenAI supports conversational refinement — you can ask the AI to pivot on a specific dimension (e.g., "make it more B2B focused", "target developing markets", "reduce capital requirements"), expand any section, or merge two ideas into a hybrid concept.
Is this suitable for IBM Hackathon submission? ▾
Absolutely. The platform was specifically architected with IBM Hackathon criteria in mind — mandatory use of IBM Langflow, IBM Orchestrate, IBM Granite Models, and IBM watsonx.ai is central to the architecture, not superficial. The system demonstrates real business value, technical depth, and innovation impact.
How accurate are the market estimates? ▾
Market estimates are AI-generated directional figures synthesized from public data sources. They are intended as starting points for further validation — not investment-grade research. We recommend using them as a compass for prioritization and then commissioning detailed research for serious business decisions.
